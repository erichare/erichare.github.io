CUGA is IBM Research's open-source framework for building enterprise-grade generalist AI agents.
It shipped publicly in December 2025 and is designed to be harness-agnostic — you bring your
own models, tools, and deployment target.

I've contributed upgrades and integration work including a LangChain dependency upgrade
([PR #145](https://github.com/cuga-project/cuga-agent/pull/145)) alongside my Langflow work at IBM.
