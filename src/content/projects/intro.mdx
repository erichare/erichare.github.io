intRo was a web-based statistical analysis tool, intended to bridge the gap between "black-box GUI
stats software" and "code-your-own-analysis in R." The design principle was
**modularity**: every statistical procedure is a self-contained module you can plug into any
teaching context. We published the architecture in *JCGS* (Hare &amp; Kaplan, 2017) and picked up
the Ron Wasserstein Award for Best Contributed Paper in Statistical Education (ASA, 2015).
